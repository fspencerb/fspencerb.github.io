About Spencer

I want to be a part of advancing technology and at the forefront of the development of that technology. I have a strong enthusiasm and passion for Web Development, Business Economics, and Customer Service.

After initially starting school for computer programming at Idaho State then onto Boise State, ended up diverting from education to employment to help pay the bills with labor intensive jobs. In the last year, I have realized how much I enjoy working in my passion and furthered my training and experience in programming. During the career transition, I completed numerous online courses on Lynda.com, codeschool.com, freecodecamp, KhanAcademy, Pluralsight and codecademy, and completed an extensive, accelerated 12-week Software Development program offered by Boise Code Works in the fall of 2015. The program gave me applicable experience that I immediately implemented in my position as a Software Testing Engineer for ChargeItPro, a financial processing company. While Boise Code Works prepared me in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, and AngularJS, I received valuable experience writing test plans and reviewing code in C# for ChargeItPro. I also worked in Process Monitor, Fiddler, Microsoft Visual Studio, Jira, Robomongo, Hyper-V Hosting, TFS, and many other programs. Examples of my work and familiarity in tools, frameworks, and languages can be found on my resume page which then links to my projects.

Code Crawl is a site that has led to my discovery of great coding and learning. And just for fun, Thanksgiving Etiquette, is one of my favorite funny videos of all time.
